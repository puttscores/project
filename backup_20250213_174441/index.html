Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Which charts can I use?

Workbird works with any real-time chart platform in your browser:

  • TradingView (Free or Premium)
  • Yahoo Finance Charts
  • Alpaca Markets Charts
  • Any web-based charting platform
What do I need to get started?

To get started with Workbird, you'll need:

  • Google Chrome browser
  • OpenRouter API key (for AI analysis)
  • Alpaca Markets API key (for trading)
  • Internet connection for real-time data

Both OpenRouter and Alpaca offer free tiers perfect for getting started.

How does the AI analysis work?

Workbird uses OpenRouter's advanced AI models to:

  • Analyze real-time price patterns and market conditions
  • Evaluate multiple technical indicators simultaneously
  • Identify potential trading opportunities
  • Calculate optimal entry and exit points
Is paper trading supported?

Yes! We strongly recommend starting with paper trading. Simply use your Alpaca paper trading API keys to:

  • Practice with real market data
  • Test strategies risk-free
  • Learn the platform safely
  • Build confidence before live trading
What AI models power Workbird?

Workbird allows you to choose from OpenRouter's vision-capable AI models. We currently recommend:

  • Llama (Our current recommended model)
  • Other vision-capable models available on OpenRouter

You can select your preferred AI model in the extension settings. The chosen model will "see" and analyze charts in real-time, just like a human trader would, but with the ability to process multiple patterns and indicators simultaneously.

Can I analyze multiple stocks simultaneously?

Currently, Workbird is optimized for single-stock analysis:

  • One stock per Chrome tab for optimal performance
  • Multiple tabs are not recommended during development
  • Use separate computers for analyzing different stocks
  • This prevents AI vision crossover between charts

While we're working on multi-stock support, we recommend focusing on one stock at a time for the most accurate analysis and to prevent any potential AI vision interference between charts.

Is signup required for Workbird?

No signup is required to download and run Workbird on your computer. However, you will need to create accounts for:

  • OpenRouter - to access AI analysis capabilities
  • Alpaca Markets - to execute trades

Both services offer free tiers and are essential for Workbird's core functionality.

How do I switch to real money trading?

To switch from paper trading to live trading:

  • Navigate to src/background.js in your file explorer
  • Change the API endpoint to: https://api.alpaca.markets/v2/orders
  • Replace paper trading API keys with your Alpaca live account keys

⚠️ Important: We strongly recommend mastering Workbird's capabilities and strategies through paper trading before switching to live trading.

Remember: Real money trading involves substantial risk. Always start small and trade responsibly. Hence, that is why we made it difficult for you to switch from paper to live trading.

What type of Pine Script should I use?

While you can use any Pine Script indicators, we provide our recommended Pattern Detector & EMA script for optimal results:

//@version=5
indicator("Pattern Detector & EMA", overlay=true)

// EMA Indicators
shortest = ta.ema(close, 20)
short = ta.ema(close, 50)
longer = ta.ema(close, 100)
longest = ta.ema(close, 200)

plot(shortest, color=color.red, title="EMA 20")
plot(short, color=color.orange, title="EMA 50")
plot(longer, color=color.aqua, title="EMA 100")
plot(longest, color=color.blue, title="EMA 200")

This script includes:

  • Multiple EMA lines (20, 50, 100, 200)
  • Pattern detection for:
    • Bullish & Bearish Flags
    • Cup and Handle
    • Head and Shoulders
    • Rising & Falling Wedges
  • Automatic pattern labeling on the chart

You can copy this script into TradingView's Pine Script editor or use your own custom indicators. Workbird's AI can analyze any visible indicators on your chart.

How do indicators affect AI analysis accuracy?

The AI's analysis accuracy improves with more visible technical indicators:

  • More visible indicators = better analysis accuracy
  • AI can process multiple indicators simultaneously
  • Recommended indicators include:
    • Moving Averages (EMA, SMA)
    • MACD and RSI
    • Volume indicators
    • Support/Resistance levels
    • Trend lines and channels

For optimal results, we recommend displaying as many relevant technical indicators as possible on your chart. The AI's vision capabilities allow it to process and correlate multiple indicators for more comprehensive analysis.
